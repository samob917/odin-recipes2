Spaghetti Squash Carbonara

Description

Treating spaghetti squash the same way as its namesake and making carbonara out of it is a healthy way to make a large meal.

This delicious recipe comes together quickly!

Ingredients

  • Large Spaghetti Squash
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pancetta
  • Yolks
  • Water
  • Black Pepper
  • Butter
  • Parmagian
  • Pecorino
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven
  2. Cut Squash
  3. Put squash on baking sheet
  4. Roast squash
  5. Cook pancetta
  6. Add egg yolks, water, and pepper and whisk
  7. Add hot squash spaghetti and mix
  8. Add cheese and salt
  9. Put back in the oven
