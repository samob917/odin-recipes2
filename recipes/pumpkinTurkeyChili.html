Pumpkin Turkey Chili

Description

Very quick, easy, and healthy pumpkin turkey chili. This recipe is low in calories but has a lot protein. It is ideal for a quick dinner during a week day!

Ingredients

  • Vegetable Oil
  • Chopped Onion
  • Green Bell Pepper
  • Yellow Bell Pepper
  • Turkey
  • Tomatoes
  • Pumpkin Puree
  • Chili Powder
  • Black Pepper
  • Salt
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Sour Cream

Steps

  1. Heat up oil and soften vegetables in it
  2. Stir in turkey until brown
  3. Mix in tomatoes and pumpkin and season. Heat until ready.
