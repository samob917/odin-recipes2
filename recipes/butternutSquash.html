Butternut Squash Soup

Description

A cheap, easy, and healthy butternut squash soup. The soup comes together quickly and looks creamy. You will likely enjoy this recipe durint the fall and winter.

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • Onion
  • Carrots
  • Cloves
  • Salt
  • Squash
  • Cider Vinegar
  • Broth
  • Maple Syrup
  • Cayenne Pepper
  • Creme Fraiche
  • Chives

Steps

  1. Gather Ingredients and Preheat Oven
  2. Roast Vegatables
  3. Cook until soft
  4. Make brown butter
  5. Immerse vegetables in stock
  6. Blend
